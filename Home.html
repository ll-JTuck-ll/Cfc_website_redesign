Code For Charlotte

Code For Charlotte brings together individuals to solve the the biggest civic problems in Charlotte!

So what is Civic Tech, anyway?

It's using technology to empower citizens and improve government operations. By bridging the gap between technology and government, we can create a 21st century government for the people, by the people. However, to make that happen it is going to take all of us. Yes, ALL of us.

We're a volunteer army comprised of citizens, designers, developers, technology geeks and citizen activists who use technology to help solve civic and social problems in our city. Yes! There is a way for everyone to contribute.
